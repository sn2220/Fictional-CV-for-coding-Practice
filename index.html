Swati Negi

Author

Swati Negi is the author of Neverevermoor , middle grade adventure story of a little girl called Saria, set in the mystical realm of Brushaville. Her next book, Pyre, a time travel historical fiction set in the 19th century India, will be out in May 2064 from Big one publishing House. A Doctorate in Environmental sciences, she transitioned to work with a giant tech company as a Full stack web developer. She lives in Europe with her adorable 2 year old son, exuberant retreiver Shasha and doting husband. You can (not) find her in most places @sheisnotinanysocialmedia.
